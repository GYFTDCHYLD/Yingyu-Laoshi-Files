Grandpa's Story
Yéye!  
Grandpa!  
Nǐ qùguò Měiguó ma?  
Did you go to America?  
Qùguò. Nàli yǒu Zìyóu Nǚshén Xiàng.  
Yes. There is the Statue of Liberty.  
Tā zài Niǔyuē.  
It's in New York.  
Nǐ qùguò Zhōngguó ma?  
Did you go to China?  
Qùguò. Nàli yǒu Chángchéng.  
Yes. There is the Great Wall.  
Tā hěn cháng.  
It's very long.  
Nǐ qùguò Fǎguó ma?  
Did you go to France?  
Qùguò. Nàli yǒu Àifěi'ěr Tiětǎ.  
Yes. There is the Eiffel Tower.  
Tā hěn gāo.  
It's very high.  
Nǐ qùguò Yīngguó ma?  
Did you go to England?  
Qùguò. Nàli yǒu Bùkèhànmǔ Gōng.  
Yes. There is Buckingham Palace.  
Nǚwáng zhù zài nàli.  
The queen lives there.  
Nǐ qùguò Àodàlìyà ma?  
Did you go to Australia?  
Qùguò. Nàli yǒu Xīní Gējùyuàn.  
Yes. There is the Sydney Opera House.  
Tā hěn tèbié.  
It's special.  
Wà, yéye!  
Wow, grandpa!  
Wǒ yě xiǎng qù!  
I want to go, too.
