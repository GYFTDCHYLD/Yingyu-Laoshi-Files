The Magic Soup  
Huānyíng lái dào móshù biǎoyǎn!  
Welcome to the magic show.
Ànzhào wǒ shuō de zuò cài.  
Cook as I say.
Ābùlākǎdábùlā!  
Abracadabra!
Qiē yī qiē.  
Chop it.
Dā dā!  
Ta-da!
Yī gēn huángguā hé yī gēn húluóbo.  
A cucumber and a carrot.
Ābùlākǎdábùlā!  
Abracadabra!
Zhǔ yī zhǔ.  
Boil it.
Dā dā!  
Ta-da!
Shì gè yángcōng.  
It's an onion.
Ābùlākǎdábùlā!  
Abracadabra!
Jiǎo yī jiǎo.  
Stir it.
Wā! Yī gè tǔdòu hé yī gè nánguā.  
Wow, a potato and a pumpkin.
Ābùlākǎdábùlā!  
Abracadabra!
Bǎ tāmen dōu fàng jìn guō lǐ jiǎobàn.  
Put them all into the pot and mix them.
Zhè shì yī wǎn shénqí de shūcài tāng!  
It's an amazing vegetable soup.
Wǒmen yīqǐ fēnxiǎng ba!  
Let's share it together.
