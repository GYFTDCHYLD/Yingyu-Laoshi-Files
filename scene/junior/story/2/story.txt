Finding Friends
Ào, wǒ cóng dàn lǐ chūlái le.  
Oh, I'm out of the egg.  
Xiàtiān dàole.  
Summer comes.  
Rénmen chuān duǎnkù hé T-xù.  
People wear shorts and T-shirts.  
Wǒ de péngyǒu zài nǎlǐ?  
Where are my friends?  
Qiūtiān dàole.  
Fall comes.  
Rénmen chuān jiákè.  
People wear jackets.  
Wǒ de péngyǒu zài nǎlǐ?  
Where are my friends?  
Dōngtiān dàole.  
Winter comes.  
Rénmen chuān dàyī.  
People wear coats.  
Wǒ de péngyǒu zài nǎlǐ?  
Where are my friends?  
Chūntiān dàole.  
Spring comes.  
Tā zhǎodào le tā de péngyǒumen.  
He finds his friends.  
Wǒmen tuō diào yīfu ba.  
Let's take off the clothes.
