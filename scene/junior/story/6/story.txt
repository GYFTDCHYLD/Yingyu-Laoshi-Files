Road Race
Wǒmen zài lùshang.  
We're on the road.
Wǒmen lái bǐsài ba.  
Let's race.
Sān... Èr... Yī... Kāishǐ!  
3... 2... 1... GO!
Wǒ de qìchē wǎng yòu zhuǎn.  
My car turns right.
Wǒ de chūzūchē wǎng zuǒ zhuǎn.  
My taxi turns left.
Wǒ de kǎchē kāijìn suìdào.  
My truck goes into the tunnel.
Wǒ de miànbāochē cóng suìdào chūlái.  
My van comes out of the tunnel.
Wǒ de gōnggòng qìchē shàngshān.  
My bus goes up the hill.
Wǒ de mótuōchē xiàshān.  
My motorcycle goes down the hill.
Wǒ hěn kuài.  
I'm fast.
Wǒ yíngle bǐsài.  
I won the race.
