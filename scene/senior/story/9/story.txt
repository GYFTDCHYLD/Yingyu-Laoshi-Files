Mountain Park
Chéngshì biàn zāng le.  
The city gets dirty.  
Wǒmen xūyào qīngjié de kōngqì.  
We need clean air.  
Wǒmen qù shāngōngyuán ba!  
Let's go to a mountain park.  
Wǒmen kěyǐ hūxī xīnxiān kōngqì  
We can have fresh air  
hé kàn měilì de fēngjǐng.  
and see a beautiful scene.  
Nàxiē rén dào chù luàn rēng lājī.  
The men throw the trash everywhere.  
Qǐng bùyào luàn rēng lājī.  
Please don't litter.  
Bǎ bōli, zhǐ hé guànzi fàng zài zhèlǐ.  
Put glass, paper and cans here.  
Bùyào làngfèi.  
Don't waste.  
Wǒmen bìxū huíshōu lìyòng.  
We have to recycle.  
Wǒmen shǐyòngle tài duō néngyuán.  
We use energy a lot.  
Wǒmen bìxū jiéyuē néngyuán.  
We have to save energy.  
Duì dàzìrǎo yào yǒu shànxīn.  
Be kind to nature.  
Dàdì xiànzài hěn měi.  
The land is beautiful, now.  
Duōme měi de dìqiú a!  
What a beautiful earth!
