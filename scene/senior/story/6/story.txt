Go To The Rainbow
Wǒmen qù cǎihóng ba!  
Let's go to the rainbow.  
Wǒmen zěnme qù ne?  
How can we go?  
Wǒmen yòng jiāotōng gōngjù ba!  
Let's use the transportation.  
Hǎo de! Dàijiàn!  
OK! See you later.  
Wǒ zài gōngqìzhàn.  
I'm at the bus terminal.  
Wǒ shì sījī.  
I'm a driver.  
Wǒ zuò gōnggòng qìchē. Shàng chē!  
I ride the bus. Get on!  
Wǒ shì huǒchē sījī.  
I'm a train driver.  
Qǐng chūshì chēpiào.  
Show me the ticket.  
Wǒ zài huǒchē zhàn.  
I'm at the train station.  
Wǒmen zài mǎtou.  
We're at the port.  
Wǒ shì chuányuán.  
I'm a sailor.  
Wǒ zuò chuán. Shàng chuán! 
I ride the ship. Get on!  
Wǒ zài jīchǎng.  
I'm at the airport.  
Wǒ shì fēixíngyuán.  
I'm a pilot.  
Wǒ zuò fēijī.  
I ride the airplane.  
Huānyíng! Huānyíng!  
Welcome! Welcome!  
Wǒ zài dìtiě zhàn.  
I'm at the subway station.  
Wǒ shì dìtiě sījī.  
I'm a subway driver.  
Wǒ zuò dìtiě. Shàng chē!  
I ride the subway. Get on!  
Wǒmen zài nǎlǐ?  
Where are we?  
Wǒmen dàodá cǎihóng le!  Xià chē! 
We arrived at the rainbow. Get off!
