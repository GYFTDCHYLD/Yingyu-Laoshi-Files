No More Junk Food

Let's have dinner.

Are you ready to order?

I don't like milk.

I don't like eggs.  
Wǒ bù xǐhuān yìdàmiàn.  
I don't like spaghetti.

I don't like bread.  
Yào jūnyín de yǐnshí.  
Have a balanced meal.  
Nǐ zhǔnbèi hǎo diǎncài le ma?  
Are you ready to order?  
Shì de! Qǐng gěi wǒ qiǎokèlì, tángguǒ hé kělè.  
Yes! Chocolate, candy and coke, please.  
Gěi nǐ.  
Here you are.  
Wā, kàn qǐlái hěn hǎochī!  
Wow, they look delicious!  
Āi! Zhè shì là de tángguǒ.  
Oh! It's a spicy candy.  
Hái yǒu xián de qiǎokèlì.  
And it's a salty chocolate.  
Āi! Zhè shì suān de língshí.  
Oh! It's a sour snack.  
Tāmen yīdiǎn yě bù tián.  
They are not sweet at all.  
Wǒ bù chī lājī shípǐn.  
I won't eat junk food.  
Nǐ shì gè hǎo háizi.  
You're a good boy.
