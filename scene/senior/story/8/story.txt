Lost Ring
Wǒ de jièzhi diū le!  
I lost my ring!  
Nǐ néng bāng wǒ zhǎo jièzhi ma?  
Can you help me find my ring?  
Wǒmen qù kètīng ba!  
Let's go to the living room!  
Zài shāfā xiàmiàn ma? Bú zài!  
Is it under the sofa? No!  
Wǒmen qù chúfáng ba!  
Let's go to the kitchen!  
Zài bīngxiāng lǐ ma? Bú zài!  
Is it in the refrigerator? No!  
Wǒmen qù xǐshǒujiān ba!  
Let's go to the bathroom!  
Zài yùgāng lǐ ma? Bú zài!  
Is it in the bathtub? No!  
Wǒmen qù yīguì zhǎo ba!  
Let's look in the dresser!  
Zài yīguì lǐ ma? Bú zài!  
Is it in the closet? No!  
Wǒmen qù wòshì ba!  
Let's go to the bedroom!  
Zài chuáng shàng ma? Bú zài!  
Is it on the bed? No!  
Māmā de jièzhi zài nǎlǐ?  
Where is mommy's ring?  
Āi! Zài wǒ de kǒudài lǐ.  
Oh! It's in my pocket.  
Duìbuqǐ.  
I'm sorry.
