Shape world
Kàn kan nàxiē sānjiǎoxíng.  
Look at the triangles.  
Tāmen shì shān.  
They're mountains.  
Bù, bù! Shì yī zhī húli.  
No, no! It's a fox.  
Yǒu jǐ zhī? Yī zhī húli.  
How many? One fox.  
Kàn kan nàge yuánquān.  
Look at the circle.  
Tā shì yuèliang.  
It's the moon.  
Bù, bù! Shì yī zhī xióng.  
No, no! It's a bear.  
Yǒu jǐ zhī? Liǎng zhī xióng.  
How many? Two bears.  
Kàn kan nàge zhèngfāngxíng.  
Look at the square.  
Tā shì yī shàn chuānghu.  
It's a window.  
Bù, bù! Shì yī kuài bǐnggān.  
No, no! It's a cracker.  
Yǒu jǐ kuài? Sān kuài xiāngtián de bǐnggān.  
How many? Three yummy crackers.  
Kàn kan nàxiē chángfāngxíng.  
Look at the rectangles.  
Tāmen shì jīmù.  
They're blocks.  
Bù, bù! Tāmen shì dàxiàng de tuǐ.  
No, no! They're elephant legs.  
Yǒu jǐ tiáo tuǐ? Sì tiáo tuǐ.  
How many? Four legs.  
Xíngzhuàng dàochù dōu shì.  
Shapes are everywhere.
